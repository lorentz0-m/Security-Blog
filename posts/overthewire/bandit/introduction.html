OverTheWire Bandit Wargame Introduction

Updated: 29/11/2024
| 0 min read

Welcome to Bandit!

This is a wargame hosted on overthewire.org. It's purpose is to teach the fundamentals required to be able to play other wargames. It is also a great way to learn how to use Linux! Beating one level means getting the password for the next one, so that's the goal we're going to have in mind. If you ever get stuck on something you don't know, be patient and continue your research.

Have fun learning!
